We're not alone and neither are you.

A better quality of life for newcomers and thier families has been a struggle we all endure, but by helping each other, we stand a strong chance of achieving that goal, for everyone. We're proud present to you this list of esteemed peers and organizations that can help you face the road ahead.

Click the logos or (Visit) to access thier sites

The Migrant Rights Network

"The Migrant Rights Network is a cross-Canada alliance to combat racism and fight for migrant justice. We are a network of self-organized migrants including farmworkers, careworkers, international students, undocumented people as well as allies." (Visit)


The Canadian Council For Refugees

"The Canadian Council for Refugees is a national non-profit umbrella organization committed to the rights and protection of refugees and other vulnerable migrants in Canada and around the world and to the settlement of refugees and immigrants in Canada." (Visit)


Canada Newcomers & Immigration Association

"Canada Newcomers & Immigration Association (CNIA) is a not-for-profit organization established to support, empower, and integrate newcomers and refugees into the Canadian society." (Visit)


VisaPlace

"VisaPlace is backed by the latest technology in immigration case processing and works with trained and experienced immigration lawyers who represent (the founder's) vision in providing the very best, most efficient, and cost-effective immigration and visa services available." (Visit)


Carewiser

"Carewiser is a Smart Daycare Spots finder. It uses an intelligent algorithm that combines information from daycare service-providers' network, user community, and publicly available data to predict an available Daycare Spot in your neighbourhood." (Visit)
